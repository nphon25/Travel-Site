Sicily

A Mediterranean mosaic of history, flavor, and adventure

About Sicily

Sicily, the soul of the Mediterranean, invites you into a vibrant tale woven with Greek legends, Roman grandeur, and volcanic drama. From the smoldering slopes of Mount Etna to the serene beaches of Cefalù, each corner is a story, every bite a legacy.

This island isn’t just a destination—it’s a layered experience of history, cuisine, landscapes, and people. Whether you're sipping Nero d’Avola on a sun-drenched terrace or wandering the Valley of the Temples at sunset, Sicily stirs something ancient within.

Top Attractions

Mount Etna

Europe’s tallest volcano offers thrilling hikes, lunar landscapes, and views that stretch beyond the Ionian coast.

Valle dei Templi

Step into antiquity at Agrigento’s ancient cityscape—an open-air museum of Doric columns and golden ruins.

Taormina Theatre

This cliffside Greco-Roman amphitheater hosts timeless performances with Mount Etna as your backdrop.

Stay & Savor

Corsaro Etna Hotel

2,000m up Mount Etna, this boutique stay offers cozy mountain comfort and unbeatable access to volcano adventures.

Rifugio Sapienza

Rustic charm meets alpine ease in this high-altitude hideaway near ski lifts and crater walks.

Cantoniera

Stop in for hearty portions of authentic Sicilian food—local wines, fresh pastas, and smoky grilled meats await.
